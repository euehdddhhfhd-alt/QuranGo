Privacy Policy

Introduction

Hi — this page explains, in plain language, what we collect about you, why we collect it, and how you can make choices about your data.

What we collect

We collect only what we need to run the site:

  • Automatic info: basic technical details like which pages you visit, your browser, and your IP address — usually used in aggregate to help improve the site.
  • Ad data: ad partners (such as Google AdSense) may use cookies or identifiers to deliver and measure ads.

Ads & cookies

We use Google AdSense to show ads. Ad partners may set cookies or use other identifiers to deliver and measure ads.

If you prefer not to see personalized ads, visit Google Ad Settings. You can also manage cookies in your browser, though that may affect how the site works for you.

We also include a small debug helper (runs in your browser only) that checks whether ad slots are filled and logs status in the developer console; it does not collect or send personal data.

Third parties

We use Google AdSense to display ads. At the moment we do not include separate analytics libraries like Google Analytics — only ad partners may collect ad-related data.

How we use your information

In short: to run the site and to make it better. Specifically, we use information to:

  • Fix bugs and improve site content and speed.
  • Show ads so we can fund hosting and development.

We may share data with service providers who help us host the site, analyze traffic, or deliver ads. These providers have their own privacy policies.

Your choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Visit Google Ad Settings to opt out of personalized advertising.
  • Block cookies: You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings (some site features may not work properly if you do this).

Security

We work to keep your information safe by using standard technical measures. While we do our best, no system is 100% secure — if you think your data has been exposed, please let us know right away.

Children

This site is not aimed at children under 13. If you believe we have collected a child’s information, tell us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make important changes, we’ll update the date at the top and post the new version here. We encourage you to check this page from time to time.
